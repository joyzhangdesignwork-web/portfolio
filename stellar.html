Women of Sci-fi —
Interactive Stellar Map

A 3D interactive globe mapping 30 women science fiction writers across a celestial canvas. Click any star to learn about the writer behind it.

About

The Idea

Women SF writers are underrepresented in most canons. I wanted to build something that celebrated them visually — a star map felt right. Each writer gets a star, and the globe invites you to explore.

The Build

Built with Three.js for the 3D globe and orbital controls. Each star is a clickable data point with writer info. The celestial aesthetic — dark background, glowing points — was intentional from the start.
